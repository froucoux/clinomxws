Key Publications

    Clinical Diagnosis of Rare Diseases using Leaky Noisy-OR Bayesian Networks

    dial.uclouvain.be

    F. Roucoux & S. Jodogne (UCLouvain / ICTEAM). Peer-reviewed paper accepted at Medical Informatics Europe (MIE) 2026, Genova, Italy. Presents a probabilistic diagnostic engine using leaky noisy-OR Bayesian networks constructed from Orphanet and HPO data.
