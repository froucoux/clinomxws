Tools & Frameworks

  • ⚙

    Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO)

    hpo.jax.org

    Standardised vocabulary of phenotypic abnormalities, used as a foundational ontology in the Clinom-X knowledge graph.

    ↗ (opens in new tab)
  • ⚙

    Orphanet — Rare Disease Database

    orphanet.org

    European reference portal for rare diseases, providing disease classifications and gene-disease associations used in graph construction.

    ↗ (opens in new tab)
  • ⚙

    SNOMED CT

    snomed.org

    Comprehensive clinical terminology used for mapping between EHR data and the knowledge graph's ontological layer.
